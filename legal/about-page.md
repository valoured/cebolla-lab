# About Cebolla

## What is Cebolla?

Cebolla is an **MLB analytics and research platform**. We aggregate
publicly available baseball data, generate statistical projections
using proprietary models, and present research tools to help users
better understand player performance, matchup context, and market
dynamics.

**All content provided on the Platform is for informational and
entertainment purposes only.** Nothing on Cebolla constitutes
financial advice, investment advice, or specific betting
recommendations.

**We are not a sportsbook.** We do not accept wagers, process
payments to gambling operators, or provide specific betting advice.

## What we provide

- **Statistical projections** for MLB players and games
- **Historical batter-vs-pitcher matchup data** built from publicly
  available historical records
- **Weather analysis** for outdoor stadiums
- **Lineup tracking** as projected lineups are released
- **AI-generated parlay analysis** ("Cebolla Cards") — research
  showing how mathematically structured parlay combinations score
  against current market lines
- **Daily POD** (Projection of the Day) — a single high-confidence
  player projection identified by our model

## What we don't provide

- ❌ Specific betting advice or "lock picks"
- ❌ Guaranteed wins or hot streaks
- ❌ Real-money wagering
- ❌ Payment processing to sportsbooks

## How we use our data

We rely on publicly available data sources including:
- MLB's public Stats API for game and player statistics
- Public weather data
- Publicly displayed sportsbook odds

We are an **independent research platform** and have no affiliation,
endorsement, or sponsorship from MLB, MLB Advanced Media, the MLB
Players Association, DraftKings, FanDuel, or any other sportsbook,
team, or league.

## Methodology

Our projections combine:
- Recent player performance (rolling windows of last 15, 50, and 100
  games)
- Statcast-derived metrics (barrel%, hard-hit%, xSLG)
- Park factors and environmental conditions
- Matchup data (batter-vs-pitcher history, platoon splits)
- Pitcher arsenal analysis (pitch type, velocity, location)

We weight these factors using statistical models. Projections are
estimates, not predictions. Past performance does not guarantee
future results.
