## 1. Introduction

Cebolla ("we", "our", "the Platform") respects your privacy. This
Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information
when you use cebolla.live.

## 2. Information We Collect

**Account Information** (when you sign up):
- Email address
- Username
- Hashed password
- Subscription tier and billing status

**Usage Data**:
- Pages and features accessed
- Search queries within the Platform
- Time spent on Platform
- Device type and browser
- IP address (used for security and analytics; anonymized where
  possible)

**Payment Information**:
- Processed exclusively through our third-party payment processor
  (e.g., Stripe).
- We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers. We retain
  only the last 4 digits and card brand for receipt purposes.

**We do NOT collect**:
- Information about your sports betting activity outside Cebolla
- Your real-money wagering history
- Your sportsbook account information

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information to:
- Provide and maintain the Platform
- Process subscriptions and payments
- Personalize content (e.g., dashboard preferences)
- Send important account notifications (security, billing)
- Improve our analytics models and Platform features
- Respond to support requests
- Detect and prevent fraud

We do **not** sell personal information to third parties.

## 4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:
- **Essential**: Authentication, session management
- **Functional**: Remember your preferences (e.g., theme, default sport)
- **Analytics**: Understand Platform usage in aggregate

You may disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings.
Disabling essential cookies may break Platform functionality.

## 5. Data Sharing

We share information only with:
- **Service Providers**: Cloud hosting (Cloudflare), database
  (Supabase), payment processing (Stripe), analytics platforms — all
  bound by contractual data protection obligations.
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law (subpoena, court
  order, law enforcement request with proper legal basis).
- **Business Transfers**: In the event of merger, acquisition, or
  sale of assets, your information may be transferred subject to
  this Policy.

We do **not** share your information with:
- Sportsbooks or gambling operators
- Marketing partners
- Data brokers

## 6. Data Retention

- **Account data**: Retained while your account is active and for up
  to 90 days after deletion for compliance and dispute resolution.
- **Payment records**: Retained for 7 years for tax and financial
  compliance.
- **Usage logs**: Aggregated and anonymized after 12 months.

## 7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
- Password hashing using industry-standard algorithms
- Restricted access to production systems
- Regular security reviews

No system is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the
confidentiality of your account credentials.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Portability**: Export your data in a structured format
- **Opt-out**: Unsubscribe from non-essential emails
- **Restriction**: Restrict certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@cebolla.live.

For California residents: We comply with CCPA. You may also request
information about categories of personal data we have collected.

For EU/UK residents: We comply with GDPR. Our legal basis for
processing is performance of contract (subscription) and legitimate
interests (Platform improvement, fraud prevention).

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for users under 18. Users engaging in
any wagering-related activity must be 21+ in most U.S. jurisdictions.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals
under 18. If we discover such collection, we will delete the
information.

## 10. International Users

The Platform is operated from the United States. By using the
Platform, you consent to your information being transferred to and
processed in the United States.

## 11. Third-Party Links

The Platform may contain links to third-party websites (e.g.,
sportsbooks, news sources). We are not responsible for the privacy
practices of these sites. Review their privacy policies before
sharing information.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes
will be notified via Platform announcement or email. Continued use of
the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance.

## 13. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: privacy@cebolla.live
General support: support@cebolla.live
